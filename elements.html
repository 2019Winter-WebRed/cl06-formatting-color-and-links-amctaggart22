Shawn Mendes

Shawn Mendes is a Canadian singer who got is start making covers of popular songs on Youtube. From Youtube, Shawn started making 6 second videos on Vine until he eventually got a record deal from Island Records.

Shawn has released three albums- Handwritten, Illuminate, and a self-titled album. In 2019, Shawn's self-titled album received two Grammy nominations. In 2018, Time named Shawn one of the year's most influential people.
